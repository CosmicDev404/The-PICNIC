An Escape!

Your family decides to take you on a picnic. You are happy, at last- after such long, tiring exams- an escape!
You enter a dark, misty, foggy, mountainous and lush green forest with trees piercing the sky. You are surprised and happy about the sight.
Your family arranges a rug on the short grass and lays down the dishes. You had a great feast. Soon, you fell into a dreamy sleep.
As soon as you wake up, it's evening but something's odd: your parents are nowhere to be found, the sky is red, and there is only one path. You are not in your universe!

What should you do to escape? Be quick, something unpleasant is watching.
